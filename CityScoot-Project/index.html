Our Story

CityScoot offers an eco-friendly, safe and fun way of seeing the sights of London. We offer tours that take in most of the best-known sites and areas of the city, including:

Soho, Westminster, Camden Town, Notting Hill, Hampstead Heath, Greenwich and Wapping.

We provide reliable safety-tested scooters and helmets. All tours are led by experienced and knowledgeable guides.

Routes

Routes are designed to take in the highlights of London. They are also designed to optimise safety. Where possible they make use of the city’s extensive cycle superhighways and cycle lanes.
