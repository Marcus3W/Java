Our Routes

CityScoot tours are designed around areas of London, such as Soho, Bloomsbury, Greenwich, Camden Town etc. Routes take in major highlights, but also explore off-the-beaten-track. Each tour takes approximately four hours to complete. There are always short stops for drinks and a longer stop for lunch or dinner. Bookings can be made up to two-hours before the tour starts.
